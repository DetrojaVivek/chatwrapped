Privacy Policy

Last Updated: {{ now.strftime('%B %d, %Y') if now else 'February 17, 2026' }}

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data to provide our services: your email address, name (for account personalization), and aggregated analysis statistics (e.g., total messages, top words). We do not use your data for ad targeting.

2. Chat Files & Content

We respect your privacy. We do NOT store your actual chat content (messages, media, voice notes). Your uploaded chat file is processed purely in memory to generate statistics and is deleted immediately after processing. Calculated statistics (numbers, counts) are stored securely to allow you to view your history.

3. Payment Information

Payments are processed securely via Razorpay. We do not store or even see your credit card details, UPI IDs, or banking information. Razorpay handles the entire transaction securely.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We do not share your data with third parties except where necessary to provide the service (e.g., payment processing).

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data instantly from your Dashboard Settings. Alternatively, you can email us at support@chatwrapped.in to request complete data deletion.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie only to maintain your login state. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.
